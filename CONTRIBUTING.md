# Contributing to the C² Method

C² is free and open — and it gets sharper when the builders who use it push back
on it. Here's how to give feedback or propose a change.

## Three ways in

- **Discuss the method** → [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/waymakerone/c2method/discussions).
  Ideas, questions, "have you considered…", debates about the approach. Start
  here when it's a conversation.
- **Report a problem or fix** → [open an issue](https://github.com/waymakerone/c2method/issues/new/choose).
  A broken or unclear template, a typo, a doc gap, a bug in the audit prompt —
  concrete and closeable.
- **Propose a change** → open a Pull Request. Edits to the templates, the
  methodology, or the quick-start are all welcome.

## What good feedback looks like

- **Be specific.** Name the file, quote the line, say what's wrong and what would
  be better.
- **Practitioner-first.** "Here's what happened when I tried it" beats "here's
  what the theory says."
- **Small and shippable** beats big and perfect.
